How to play

Fill every cell 1–n so each row, each column, and each stream (a chain of beads on one thread) holds every number exactly once.

Tap a bead, then a number. Tap the same number or Erase to clear. Stuck? Hint reveals the next logical step. No fail state — clashes just glow.
